Performance Advertising

Performance Advertising is a type of digital marketing wherein the advertisers decide to have a specific activity done through advertising. Once the pre-decided activity is done, only then do they pay the marketing channel and also pre-decided cost. Hence, as the name suggests, this marketing relies completely on the performance of the advertisement. With the outburst of technology and the influence of the internet on the world, performance advertising is taking deeper roots. Because it is possible to track every single activity of a user, it is also easy to track the preferences, opinions, and habits of that user. Having known that, targeting the audience becomes easier and that’s how performance advertising becomes successful.

Performance Measurement

If you cannot measure it, it is necessarily not even happening! That’s true! But Performance Advertising offers you a number of parameters that you can easily understand and use to measure the performance of your advertising. Here are a few of them:

  • Cost per Click (CPC)- It is the amount that the advertiser has to pay only when the advertisement is clicked on.
  • Cost per Impression (CPM)- It is the amount that is paid by the advertiser every one thousand times the advertisement is clicked on.

  • Cost per Sales (CPS)- It is the amount that is paid by the advertiser if a sale is generated through the advertisement.
  • Cost per Lead (CPL)- It is an amount paid by the advertiser when the advertisement gets a CTA (call to action). For example: A sign up.
  • Cost per Acquisition (CPA)- It is an amount paid by the advertiser when a sale occurs.
